# Summary

These are SolidWorks and STEP files I designed for the laser-cut acrylic chassis of my line-following robot, <a href="https://www.pololu.com/blog/487/brandons-line-following-robot-the-chariot">The Chariot</a>, for the <a href="https://www.pololu.com/blog/493/video-lvbots-april-2015-line-following-competition">2015 Las Vegas robotics club line following competition</a>.

The dimensions below correspond to the assembled acrylic chassis (without any other components):
    Height: 49 mm
    Length: 131 mm
    Width: 80 mm
